Welcome to the Aumorean Federation's planet network

The Aumorean Federation is a loose network of a few dozen planets and moons (and many more mining colonies) in a small cluster of the galaxy. The people of these planets have committed to peaceful trade and freedom of movement, enforced by an interplanetary fleet of police ships. While space travel remains dangerous, the emergence of the federation has created a semblance of stability in the area, keeping pirates on their toes.

You're the captain of a small cargo ship, and you're looking for riches and fame. Now is the time to go on an adventure!
